# Product Overview

## Mission

Build a privacy-conscious and compliance-aware platform that helps adults discover verified cannabis social clubs in Spain, learn local norms, and complete safe membership request flows.

## Core User Groups

- Visitors looking for safe, legal-context guidance and trusted clubs.
- Local members managing favorites, requests, and profile activity.
- Club operators managing inbound requests, profile settings, and dashboard workflows.

## Current Product Areas

- Multi-language public experience under `app/[lang]`.
- Club discovery and detail pages (`spain`, `clubs`, neighborhood/city routes).
- Editorial and safety content (`learn`, `editorial`, guides).
- Member account and profile flows (`account`, `profile`, requests, notifications).
- Club dashboard (`club-panel`) for request handling and operations.

## Product Principles

- Education first: safety/legal context is part of the product, not an afterthought.
- Trust by design: avoid misleading discovery and prioritize verified workflows.
- Progressive depth: anonymous discovery, richer functionality after authentication.
