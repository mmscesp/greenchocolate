### Before We Start: Set Your Expectations

This is not a quick process. It is not guaranteed. Respect is not optional.

---

### Step 1: Research Before You Fly

Do this before arrival, not same day.

- Identify clubs that accept international visitors
- Check requirements and response times
- Confirm language support
- Keep backups

Start from [the directory](/en/clubs/).

---

### Step 2: The Application Process

Use verified channels only. Never street promoters or random social DMs.

Prepare real identity details and wait for formal response. Rejection is possible and normal.

---

### Step 3: Preparing for Your First Visit

Bring:
- Valid ID
- Membership confirmation
- Cash (where needed)

Do not bring non-member guests or expectations from other countries.

---

### Step 4: Arrival and Intake

Follow intake instructions exactly. Read house rules fully. Ask staff if anything is unclear.

---

### Step 5: Inside

Respect privacy, keep your phone away, and follow staff guidance.

Treat the contribution model as private-association protocol, not retail behavior.

---

### Step 6: Leaving

Do not transition private behavior into public exposure.

- No public consumption
- No location sharing
- No social posting of club details

---

### Common First-Timer Mistakes

- No confirmed membership before going
- Following strangers for access
- Taking photos inside
- Public consumption after leaving

Read [Barcelona vs. Amsterdam](/en/editorial/barcelona-vs-amsterdam-cannabis) and [The Safety Kit](/en/editorial/safety-kit-visitors-spain) before your first attempt.
