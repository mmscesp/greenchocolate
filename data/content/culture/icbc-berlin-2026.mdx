ICBC Berlin is a business-first conference. It is designed for people who care about regulation, market development, partnerships, and operational strategy more than general consumer culture.

## What to Expect

The conference typically attracts operators, investors, policy watchers, legal advisers, and teams following the next phase of European cannabis regulation. That makes it useful if your questions are commercial or regulatory rather than recreational.

## Who Gets the Most Value

This is usually a strong fit for:

- operators looking at expansion or partnerships
- policy and compliance teams tracking market signals
- investors and advisers watching how regulation is shaping execution

If your goal is pure consumer discovery, this is not the right lens.

## How to Use the Event Well

The event pays off when you arrive with a sharp agenda.

- book meetings early
- track official schedule changes
- choose sessions that match your role instead of trying to attend everything

The quality of your follow-up matters more than the size of your badge stack.
