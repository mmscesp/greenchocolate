Spannabis remains one of the reference names in the European cannabis event calendar. If you are planning to attend the Bilbao edition, the useful mindset is preparation first, hype second.

## Why People Pay Attention to Spannabis

The event sits at the intersection of industry visibility, product culture, networking, and broader market signaling. Even people who do not attend closely watch what brands, conversations, and priorities show up there.

## How to Plan the Trip

The basics matter more than people admit.

- book travel and accommodation early
- monitor official channels for updates
- confirm venue logistics before arrival
- separate event planning from assumptions about local cannabis rules

## On-Site Conduct Still Matters

Event space rules and city-level legal reality are not the same thing. A conference badge does not change local enforcement logic, and a permissive atmosphere inside an expo does not mean careless public behavior outside is safe.

Treat the event as a professional environment first and a spectacle second.
