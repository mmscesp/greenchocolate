## Short Version

Spain does not run a public legal-retail cannabis system. Public possession and consumption can still trigger administrative sanctions.

## Public vs Private

The most important distinction is where behavior occurs. Public-space behavior has significantly higher risk exposure.

## CSC Context

CSCs operate in a legal gray area with private-association logic. They are not equivalent to open retail venues.

## Visitor Rule

Assume enforcement can vary by place and moment. Do not rely on hearsay or tourist myths.
