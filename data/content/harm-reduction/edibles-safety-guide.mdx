<AnswerBox>
  Edibles are processed by the liver, converting THC into 11-hydroxy-THC, which is more potent and longer-lasting than smoking. **Start low (5-10mg)** and **wait at least 90 minutes** before taking more. Most "bad trips" happen because people get impatient and double-dose.
</AnswerBox>

## The "Creep" Effect
When you smoke, you feel it instantly. When you eat cannabis, it must be digested.
*   **Onset:** 30 minutes to 2 hours.
*   **Peak:** 2 to 4 hours.
*   **Duration:** 4 to 8+ hours.

**The Trap:** You eat a gummy. 45 minutes later, you feel nothing. You say "This isn't working" and eat another one. 30 minutes later, the first one hits... then the second one hits. You are now in for a very intense 6 hours.

## Dosing Guide (Start Low!)
*   **1-2.5mg:** Microdose. Very mild relief. Good for first-timers.
*   **5mg:** Standard low dose. Noticeable relaxation.
*   **10mg:** Standard dose. Euphoria, impaired coordination. Strong for beginners.
*   **20mg+:** High dose. Possible anxiety, paranoia, heavy sedation.

<WarningBox title="Label Accuracy">
  In unregulated markets (or grey zones), edible labels can be inaccurate. A brownie might say "50mg" but contain 100mg, or vice-versa. Always assume it is stronger than it says until you know the specific brand/club.
</WarningBox>

## What if I get too high?
It happens. You are not dying. No one has ever died from a cannabis overdose.
1.  **Don't Panic:** It will end. It just takes time.
2.  **Hydrate:** Water helps. Avoid alcohol.
3.  **Chew Peppercorns:** Black pepper contains terpenes (caryophyllene) that can help reduce THC anxiety.
4.  **Sleep:** If you can, just sleep it off.
